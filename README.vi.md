## Tinh nang noi bat

### 1. Phoi hop: AI va con nguoi xay dung cung nhau

Coding agent co day du CLI va skill, trong khi con nguoi co giao dien WYSIWYG. Ca hai co the phoi hop hieu qua trong cung mot he thong.

#### Xay dung voi AI coding agent ma ban da quen thuoc

Tu buoc trien khai den mot he thong co the su dung duoc chi trong vai phut voi cac agent pho bien.

- Tuong thich voi cac agent pho bien nhu Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, OpenCode va TRAE
- Agent co the dam nhan setup, phat trien, di chuyen va phat hanh tu dau den cuoi

![coding-agent](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/coding-agent.png)

#### Tu xay dung trong giao dien no-code WYSIWYG

Ngay ca khi khong dung AI, ban van co the tao va dieu chinh moi thu truc quan trong giao dien visual.

- Chuyen doi giua che do su dung va che do cau hinh chi voi mot cu nhap
- Xem va cau hinh mo hinh du lieu, trang, workflow va quyen han bang giao dien truc quan
- Duoc thiet ke cho nguoi dung nghiep vu, khong chi danh cho lap trinh vien

![wysiwyg](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/wysiwyg.gif)

#### Ket hop phat trien bang AI va xay dung thu cong theo cach ban muon

Chia cong viec linh hoat: con nguoi chinh sua nhung gi AI tao, va AI tiep tuc dua tren nhung gi con nguoi da cau hinh.

- AI co the nhanh chong tao mo hinh du lieu, trang va workflow
- Con nguoi co the nhanh chong tinh chinh UI va tuong tac
- Hop tac khi can va tiep tuc lap lai de hoan thien

![ai-no-coding](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/ai-no-coding.png)

### 2. Thong minh: AI giup van hanh nghiep vu, khong chi dung de xay he thong

NocoBase tich hop nhan vien AI de AI co the lam viec truc tiep ben trong he thong.

#### Nhan vien AI duoc tich hop vao workflow nghiep vu

Nhan vien AI tu dong nhan biet boi canh nghiep vu va thuc thi tac vu ngay trong he thong.

- O front-end, ho tro phan tich, hoi dap, dien bieu mau va nhieu viec khac
- O back-end, tu dong xu ly nhan dang tai lieu, giam sat rui ro va dieu phoi tac vu
- Tich hop voi workflow, nhan vien AI co the tham gia ra quyet dinh va thuc thi

![AI-employee](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/ai-employee-home.png)

#### Giao dien mo cho he sinh thai agent

MCP, HTTP API, CLI va he skill phong phu cho phep agent ben ngoai ket noi an toan.

- Cac nen tang nhu OpenClaw, Hermes, Dify, Coze va n8n ket noi qua cac giao thuc tieu chuan
- Ket noi voi Telegram, WhatsApp, Slack va Gmail de truy van du lieu, kich hoat hanh dong va thuc thi workflow kinh doanh
- Mot mo hinh giao tiep giu cho agent ben trong va ben ngoai nam trong cung mot gioi han

![agents](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/f-agents-logos.jpeg)

#### Co che phan quyen giu hanh vi AI trong tam kiem soat

Moi hanh dong cua AI deu tuan theo cung mot he thong quyen chi tiet nhu nguoi dung thuc.

- Moi nhan vien AI co role rieng, voi quyen doc va ghi den cap truong
- Nhat ky kiem toan giup moi thay doi du lieu va moi lan kich hoat workflow deu co the truy vet
- Quan tri vien co the dieu chinh quyen AI bat cu luc nao de giu ranh gioi ro rang

![permission](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/f-permission.png)

### 3. Dang tin cay: ha tang san sang cho nghiep vu thuc te

Model du lieu, quyen han va workflow rat phuc tap va de sai sot.  
NocoBase cung cap san tat ca nhu mot lop ha tang tich hop, da duoc kiem chung trong moi truong van hanh.

#### Ha tang day du, khong can bat dau lai tu dau

Hang chuc module tich hop san bao phu nhung nhu cau pho bien nhat cua he thong nghiep vu.

- Data models, permissions, workflows, and audit logs work out of the box
- Proven in production, instead of regenerated as black-box code each time
- Built-in guardrails keep AI output aligned with the system architecture

![core](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/f-core.png)

#### Van hanh theo model du lieu, tach biet du lieu va giao dien

Du lieu nghiep vu duoc giu trong cau truc quan he tieu chuan, tach rieng khoi lop giao dien.

- Use the main database, external databases, and third-party APIs as data sources
- AI and people work on the same data model, so results stay transparent
- Your data always stays in your own database, without platform lock-in

![model](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/model.png)

#### Kien truc plugin de phat trien ben vung

Voi thiet ke microkernel, moi kha nang deu co the mo rong qua plugin ma van giu duoc tinh nhat quan.

- New features are added through composable plugins with shared conventions
- Mix custom and official plugins to fit your business
- The same architecture applies to both AI-built and manually built plugins

![plugins](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/plugins.png)

## Ket noi AI Agent

Neu ban muon AI Agent tham gia truc tiep vao viec xay dung va van hanh NocoBase, cach don gian nhat la cai dat NocoBase CLI, hoan tat khoi tao, roi khoi dong hoac khoi dong lai phien agent ngay trong thu muc do.

- NocoBase CLI dung de cai dat, ket noi va quan ly ung dung NocoBase
- Trong qua trinh khoi tao, CLI tu dong cai NocoBase Skills de agent hieu mo hinh du lieu, trang, workflow, quyen han va plugin
- Sau khi khoi tao xong, AI Agent co the bat dau lam viec ngay neu workspace tro den thu muc nay

```bash
npm install -g @nocobase/cli@beta
mkdir my-nocobase && cd my-nocobase
nb init --ui
cd my-nocobase && codex
```

Chi tiet:  
https://docs.nocobase.com/cn/ai/quick-start
